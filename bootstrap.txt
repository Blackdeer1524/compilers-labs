`axiom Production   `is Axiom NonTerm "`is" Rule RuleAlt "`end" Production 
                    `or `epsilon `end

Rule        `is RuleItem RuleTail 
            `or `epsilon `end

RuleItem    `is NonTerm 
            `or Term `end

RuleTail    `is Rule 
            `or `epsilon `end

RuleAlt     `is "`or" Rule RuleAlt
            `or `epsilon `end

Axiom       `is "`axiom"
            `or `epsilon `end

NonTerm `is "#IDENT" `end

Term    `is "#QStr" `end
