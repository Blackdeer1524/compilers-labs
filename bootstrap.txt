`axiom Production   `is Axiom NonTerm "`is" Rule RuleAlt "`end" Production 
                    `or `epsilon `end

Rule        `is Term RuleTail 
            `or NonTerm RuleTail 
            `or "`epsilon" `end

RuleTail    `is Term RuleTail 
            `or NonTerm RuleTail 
            `or `epsilon `end

RuleAlt     `is "`or" Rule RuleAlt
            `or `epsilon `end

Axiom       `is "`axiom"
            `or `epsilon `end

NonTerm `is "#Ident" `end

Term    `is "#QuotedStr" `end
